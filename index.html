To provide workout tracking and progression systems, we may collect:

We may collect information about how you interact with the app, including:

  • Diagnostic data

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Save workout and progression data
  • Enable account authentication and cloud syncing
  • Deliver premium subscription features
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Analyze app engagement and feature usage
  • Provide AI-powered coaching and workout insights
  • Respond to support requests

Third-Party Services

GymSaga may use trusted third-party services to operate the app, including services for:

  • Authentication
  • Cloud storage and databases
  • Subscription management
  • Analytics
  • Crash reporting
  • AI-powered features

These providers may process limited information necessary to provide their services.

AI-Powered Coaching and Insights

GymSaga offers optional AI-powered coaching, workout analysis, and fitness insights.

When you choose to use these features, GymSaga may transmit certain workout-related information to OpenAI, LLC for processing, including:

  • Exercise names
  • Workout history
  • Sets and repetitions
  • Weight lifted
  • Workout duration
  • Fitness progression statistics
  • Information necessary to generate coaching recommendations and workout insights

This information is used solely to generate personalized coaching feedback, workout recommendations, fitness insights, and related AI-powered features within GymSaga.

No workout data is transmitted to OpenAI for AI processing until the user has explicitly consented to the use of AI-powered features within the app.

OpenAI processes this information on GymSaga's behalf for the purpose of generating AI responses. GymSaga does not sell personal information to third parties.

Users may revoke their consent for AI-powered features at any time through the app settings. Revoking consent will prevent future workout data from being transmitted for AI processing.

For additional information regarding OpenAI's privacy practices, please refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

Information may be shared only with service providers necessary to operate GymSaga, including infrastructure, analytics, authentication, subscription, and AI functionality providers.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide the app and related services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards designed to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights & Choices

You may:

  • Stop using the app at any time
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Request deletion of stored data
  • Contact us regarding privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

GymSaga is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of GymSaga after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

When reporting an issue, include your device model, OS version, and app version if possible — it helps us fix things faster.

Before You Email

Most issues can be resolved with a quick fix. Try these steps first:

Check for Updates

Make sure GymSaga is up to date in the App Store or Google Play. Many bugs are fixed in the latest version.

Restart the App

Force quit GymSaga completely, then reopen it. This clears temporary glitches and refreshes your session.

Restart Your Device

If the app keeps crashing or acting up, a full device restart often resolves background issues.

Check Your Connection

GymSaga needs internet for syncing, login, and cloud saves. Try switching between Wi‑Fi and mobile data.

Update Your OS

Running an outdated version of iOS or Android can cause compatibility issues. Check for system updates in your device settings.

Still Stuck?

Email us at gymsagasupport@gmail.com with a description of what happened and we'll take it from there.
