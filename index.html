Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Welcome to GymSaga.

GymSaga (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information when using the GymSaga mobile application and related services.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account or sign in, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Authentication information
  • Account identifiers

Fitness & Workout Data

To provide workout tracking and progression systems, we may collect:

  • Workout history
  • Exercises completed
  • Sets, reps, weight, and duration
  • Progression statistics
  • Fitness-related in-app data

Usage Data

We may collect information about how you interact with the app, including:

  • Feature usage
  • Session activity
  • App interactions
  • Gameplay progression
  • Device information

Purchases & Subscriptions

If you purchase premium features or subscriptions, we may collect:

  • Purchase status
  • Subscription entitlement information
  • Transaction metadata

GymSaga does not directly store full payment card information.

Diagnostics & Performance Data

To improve stability and reliability, we may collect:

  • Crash logs
  • Error reports
  • Device performance information
  • Diagnostic data

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Save workout and progression data
  • Enable account authentication and cloud syncing
  • Deliver premium subscription features
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Analyze app engagement and feature usage
  • Provide AI-powered coaching and workout insights
  • Respond to support requests

Third-Party Services

GymSaga may use trusted third-party services to operate the app, including services for:

  • Authentication
  • Cloud storage and databases
  • Subscription management
  • Analytics
  • Crash reporting
  • AI-powered features

These providers may process limited information necessary to provide their services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

Information may be shared only with service providers necessary to operate GymSaga, including infrastructure, analytics, authentication, subscription, and AI functionality providers.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide the app and related services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards designed to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights & Choices

You may:

  • Stop using the app at any time
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Request deletion of stored data
  • Contact us regarding privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

GymSaga is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of GymSaga after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
